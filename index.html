Communicate with ease

Teamspeak 3 and Discord

At GLR, we use Teamspeak 3 to communicate with other members of the emergency services. All of the communication is centralised in one place making it easy to talk to each other. Everything in roleplay is discussed in the Teamspeak.

Discord is used out of character (OOC). Its mainly used to communicate with other members of the community. We also post useful infomation and announcements so look out for those. You can also find the different rules that everyone is required to follow.

A good experience

A unique expectation of excellence

At GLR we expect to put 100% into their roleplay scenes 100% of the time. Holding everyone to these expectations helps improve the experience for everyone.

Lots of our members have real world knowledge and experience within certain areas. These members help newer and less knowledgable members fit into the community more and help them to learn.

Special Events

A premium experience like no other.

GLR holds regular events and training for civillians and other divisions. This is done to heighten the experience and ability of players. These events normally consist of administration allowing certain senarios that wouldn't normally be allowed.

Server Assets

Unique assets and scripts.

GLR only hand picks assets to keep a good balance between performance and quality. We belive that a good way to improve the player experience is to give them the tools to do so. Take the wheel of our many civillian or police cars. Or take to the sky from our selection of planes.
